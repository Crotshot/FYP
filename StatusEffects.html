Status Effects

A status effect is a condition that changes the stats, position or control of a character. Effects that positively alter a character are called Buffs and ones that negatively alter a character are called De-Buffs.

Regular Effects – Ability applied from Conquerors and Minions

Stunned Disabled for duration
Speed Boosted +(Base * %) faster for duration
Slowness -(Base * %) slower for duration
Burning On fire and taking X burn damage per tick
Weakened Taking % bonus damage
Abduction Disabled and is being moved against their will
Rooted Cannot move but can still attack
Magnetised Being pulled toward location slowly

Building Effects – Applied from buildings

Wheat field 10% minion max health bonus
Pumpkin Patch 15% bonus minion ability damage
Iron Mine 10% minion melee damage
Fletcher 10% minion ranged damage
Brewery + 1% minion health regen per second
Sacred Crystal 10% Conqueror Cooldown Reduction (Diminishing)
Horde increase +3 minion horde increase for Conqueror
Charged Minion 15% move speed and 10% damage for duration
Drained Minion -15% move speed and -5% damage for duration

Unique Effects

Unique effects are effects that are applied from some Conqueror abilities that do not fit in with the regular effects as they are unique to the Conqueror.
